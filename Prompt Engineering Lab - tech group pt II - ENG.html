API Documentation #### `GET /public_transport/city/{city}/closest_departures` Retrieves the closest public transport departures that move the user towards their specified destination. **Description:** This endpoint returns a list of upcoming public transport departures from stops near the user's starting location. The departures are filtered to include only those lines that are generally heading towards the user's destination (lines starting in the opposite direction are excluded). The results are sorted by the distance of the departure stop from the `start_coordinates`, with the closest stops listed first. Note that this endpoint identifies departures heading in the correct general direction but does not guarantee that a specific line is the most optimal or direct route to the `end_coordinates`. ---
